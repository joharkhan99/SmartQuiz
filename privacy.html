Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Welcome to Smart Quiz. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app works and what data (if any) we collect.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Smart Quiz does not collect or transmit any personal information. You do not need to create an account or log in to use the app.

2. Local Storage Only

All your gameplay data (such as quiz scores, progress, and settings) is stored locally on your device. We do not send any of this data to a server or cloud storage.

3. No Backend or Server

Smart Quiz operates entirely on your device. There is no backend server, database, or cloud service involved.

4. No Advertising

We do not display any ads, banners, or in-app promotions. The app is completely free and ad-free.

5. Third-Party Services

Smart Quiz does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. No AI or Machine Learning

We do not use any artificial intelligence, large language models, machine learning, or related technologies in the app. No data collected (even locally) is used to train or improve any models.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages. Because we do not collect personal information, no parental consent is required.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with a revised “Last updated” date.
